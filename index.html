01 / The Designer

CHIRANTHA DEWRUWAN

"Minimalism is not the lack of something. It is the perfect amount of something."

I am a visual designer dedicated to the pursuit of pure, intentional design. As the creative force behind MARQUE DESIGN, I partner with forward-thinking businesses and luxury brands to build deep-rooted visual identities.

My philosophy honors structural grids, geometric precision, and the elegant power of negative space. By dismantling unnecessary distractions, I reveal the raw, singular essence of each brand—ensuring it makes its maximum impact with minimal noise.
